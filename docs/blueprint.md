# **App Name**: Animal Life Express

## Core Features:

- Local SEO & Meta Optimization: Dynamic generation and embedding of optimized meta titles, descriptions, and H-tag content including local keywords ('veterinaria en Nuevo Poeta Lugones', 'veterinaria en Córdoba') to maximize search engine visibility.
- Conversion-Focused Hero Section: Interactive hero section featuring clear value proposition, urgent messaging, and prominent WhatsApp call-to-action for immediate contact.
- Detailed Services and Benefits Display: Clear, scannable sections outlining veterinary services (Castraciones, Cirugías, Peluquería, Atención Clínica, Urgencias) with customer-centric benefits highlighted through bullet points and descriptive subheadings.
- AI-Powered Copywriting Tool: An integrated AI tool for generating A/B test variations of conversion-focused copy for CTAs, benefits, and FAQ answers, emphasizing urgency and personalized care ('Atención inmediata', 'Veterinaria cerca tuyo') based on defined parameters.
- Interactive Location and Map Integration: Embeds an interactive Google Map displaying Animal Life's location at Damian Garat 2630, X5008AHO Córdoba, with prominent display of address and operating hours including emergency services.
- Persistent WhatsApp & Phone CTAs: Implementation of multiple strategic calls-to-action across the landing page, including a floating WhatsApp button, to funnel user queries directly to '0351 232-3695' via 'Solicitar turno por WhatsApp' or 'Atención inmediata'.
- Analytics and Event Tracking: Integration with Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to track critical conversion events such as WhatsApp clicks, phone number clicks, scroll depth, and general user interactions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A serene and trustworthy blue (#2E96C2) to evoke professionalism and calm in animal care. HSL(190, 60%, 45%).
- Background Color: A clean, light off-white with a hint of blue (#ECF4F7) for a spacious and modern feel. HSL(190, 15%, 95%).
- Accent Color: A deep, rich forest green (#0F6347) for strong visual contrast, symbolizing health and nature. HSL(160, 80%, 30%).
- Headlines and Body Text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly legible characteristics, ensuring quick information absorption on any device.
- Use a set of modern, minimalist line-art icons that subtly communicate services and benefits without distracting from the core message, aligned with a professional aesthetic.
- Mobile-First Responsive Design: Optimized for seamless viewing and interaction across all devices, with an intuitive single-page scroll layout, clear information hierarchy, and ample whitespace for readability. The WhatsApp button will be consistently visible and floating.
- Subtle Micro-interactions: Incorporate smooth, tasteful animations for button hovers, section transitions, and the floating WhatsApp button to enhance user engagement without impeding page performance or conversion goals.
